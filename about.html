Yoabab Makes his fever dreams come to life!

Howdy, I am yoabab! I am a man with a plan written on the back of his hand and will not stop until you've met my demands.

In other news I am an artist who has had a deep passion for art since I was a little spud. I was the kid with doodles on everyhting. From the margins of my homework to my midterms their were always drawings. I very much like creating things with these mits and being able to share what I make with others brings me so much joy. One thing you should know about me is that I do things ass backwards. I like to use dated software for digital stuff and create physical art in ways other would say are inefficient. I fucking love mspaint and have thousands of hours spent making crazy shit. I have always used mspaint and as I got older and new programs came out none have effected me quite like mspaint.
